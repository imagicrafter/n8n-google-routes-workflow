Research Methodology & Sources: The assumptions used in this ROI calculator are based on extensive peer-reviewed academic research and industry studies. The 20% time efficiency improvements are documented by Transportation Research journals, Aberdeen Group studies, and field service management research from companies like Badger Maps and TOMRA. The 80% planning time reduction is supported by Routific platform data, Saia Logistics implementations, and McKinsey automation research. Additional job capacity estimates (2-3 jobs per vehicle monthly) are validated by University of Alberta productivity research and Aberdeen Group field service optimization studies. Sources include research from the American Transportation Research Institute (ATRI), Federal Highway Administration (FHWA), IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems, and consulting analysis from McKinsey & Company, Deloitte, PwC, and Accenture. Industry validation comes from trade associations including the National Association of Landscape Professionals (NALP), Air Conditioning Contractors of America (ACCA), Plumbing-Heating-Cooling Contractors Association (PHCC), International Sanitary Supply Association (ISSA), and National Pest Management Association (NPMA).
